Long run options for your new roof

At Roof Me we only use high quality Roofing products from the COLORSTEEL® and COLORCOTE® Range. Roofing products may look the same in appearance but they may not perform the same due to different grades (thickness), coatings (paint), profile and even what type of metallic make up they are. All our materials are backed by our Suppliers and also carry their own manufacture warranties. When choosing a Roofing Product there are many factors that you need to consider the products effectiveness, durability, safety, life expectancy of the Roof – we will make sure we quote the right materials are being used to complete the job to the highest possible standards. We have options available for every budget as affordability is also a main factor. Let us help you find the perfect Roofing Solution for your Home.
